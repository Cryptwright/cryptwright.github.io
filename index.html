Chris Valentine

This is the homepage of the bestselling RPG author and gamemaster.

I scribble grimsical adventures in the heart of the Sonoran Desert, Phoenix, Arizona, USA. I try to make sure they are fun, memorable, and make you think. I focus on rich stories, well-developed lore, and clever twists.

About Me


I've been involved with the hobby for over thirty years. At the end of 2019, I decided I wanted to start creating content. I took a few workshops and classes and began creating. So far, things are going well. Several of my titles have achieved bestselling status and some have even won awards.

I also regularly volunteer as a convention DM to help bring new people into the hobby. In addition, I'm the Vice President of Phoenix RPGs, a non-profit organization dedicated to spreading awareness of RPGs and using skills learned in-game to improve real-life problem solving, socializing, storytelling, and leadership abilities.
